\subsection{Executive Summary}\label{executive-summary}

The Lux crypto stack demonstrates \textbf{solid foundational security}
with proper use of established libraries (circl, blst, decred).
Post-quantum readiness is well-advanced with ML-DSA, ML-KEM, and SLH-DSA
implementations. However, several issues require attention:
